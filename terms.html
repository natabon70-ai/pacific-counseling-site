2 Website Purpose

3 No Therapeutic Relationship

Use of this website, including submitting a contact form or sending an email, does not establish a therapist-client relationship.

A therapeutic relationship is established only after all of the following:

  1. Completion of required intake documentation
  2. Signed informed consent
  3. An initial session has occurred
  4. Both parties mutually agree to engage in services

4 Geographic & Licensure Limitation

Clinical services are provided only to individuals physically located in the State of New Hampshire at the time of service, unless otherwise permitted by law.

5 Emergency & Crisis Disclaimer

This website and associated email accounts are not monitored for emergency or crisis communications.

If you are experiencing an emergency, please:

  • Call 911
  • Call or text 988 — Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
  • Go to your nearest emergency room

Do not use this website or email for urgent mental health concerns.

6 Email & Electronic Communication Notice

Email and website contact forms are not guaranteed to be secure or HIPAA-compliant methods of communication.

By choosing to communicate via email or website form, you acknowledge and accept the potential risks to confidentiality associated with electronic communication.

Sensitive or urgent clinical information should not be transmitted via standard email.
